HTML Character Entities

  • & ampersand & amp;
  • " double quotation mark & quot;
  • ' single quotation mark & apos;
  • ¢ cent & cent;
  • £ pound & pound;
  • ¥ yen & yen;
  • € euro & euro;
  • © copyright & copy;
  • ® registered trademark & reg;
